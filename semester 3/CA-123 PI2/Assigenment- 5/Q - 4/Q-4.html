About The Bookworm Bookshop

Since 1993, The Bookworm Bookshop has offered rane and hard-to-fund titles for the discerning reader. The Bookworm offer:

  • Freindly, Knowledgeable, and courteous help
  • Free cofee and juice for our costumers
  • A well-lit reading room so you can "try before you buy"
  • Four friendly cats: Esmerelda, Catherine, Dulcinea and Beatrice

Our hours are 10am to 9pm Weekdays. noon to 7 on weekends.
